## 📌 Project Overview

The **Restaurant Success Analytics** project focuses on analyzing restaurant performance, customer preferences, pricing behavior, cuisine popularity, and online delivery adoption using the **Zomato Dataset**.

The project applies **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**, statistical techniques, data visualization, and feature engineering to uncover meaningful insights that can help restaurant owners and food delivery platforms make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze restaurant distribution across cities.
- Identify the most popular cuisines.
- Study customer ratings and satisfaction.
- Evaluate the impact of online delivery.
- Analyze restaurant pricing patterns.
- Explore relationships between business variables.
- Generate business insights and recommendations.

## 📈 Key Findings

### 🏙️ Restaurant Distribution

- New Delhi contains the highest number of restaurants.
- NCR region dominates the dataset.

### 🍛 Cuisine Popularity

- North Indian cuisine is the most popular.
- Chinese and Fast Food follow closely.

### 🚚 Online Delivery Impact

- Delivery-enabled restaurants receive higher ratings.
- Delivery-enabled restaurants receive more customer votes.

### 💰 Pricing Insights

- Most restaurants belong to Budget and Moderate categories.
- Luxury restaurants achieve the highest average ratings.

### ⭐ Customer Ratings

- Majority of restaurants fall into Average and Good categories.
- Higher customer engagement generally leads to better ratings.
